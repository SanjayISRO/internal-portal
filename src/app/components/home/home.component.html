The Genesys India Hackathon 2023 is an exciting initiative aimed at enabling all Genesys India employees to align themselves with the organization's strategy by exploring the capabilities of the Genesys Cloud product. The hackathon's main purpose is to foster collaboration among cross-functional teams and create an atmosphere of innovation and creativity. The caption "Connect", "Collaborate", "Create", and "Conquer" emphasizes the portal's purpose of facilitating connections and collaborations among employees and empowering them to create innovative solutions.

The hackathon's portal provides employees with a platform to connect and collaborate with one another. Individuals interested in connecting can enroll themselves by mentioning their skill set or domain knowledge, while teams seeking specific skill sets can enroll about their teams and the skill sets they are looking for. This platform empowers employees to collaborate and share ideas with individuals from different functional units, thereby gaining new perspectives and insights.

In addition to the collaboration platform, the key highlight of the hackathon is the series of enablement workshops on various topics related to the Genesys Cloud products. These workshops are designed to provide employees with the basic knowledge and skills about the Genesys Cloud product and from there on we can explore further and create innovative solutions. The recorded workshop videos are also made available to all employees under the Workshops section for reference and learning purposes.

The Genesys India Hackathon 2023 is an excellent opportunity for all employees to come together and collaborate towards achieving the organization's goals. By connecting and collaborating with one another, employees can leverage their skills and domain knowledge to create innovative solutions that can help the organization conquer new heights. The hackathon's success will depend on the active participation of all employees, as together, they can achieve more and create a better future for themselves and the organization.
